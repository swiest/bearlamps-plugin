A Bear Lamp eXtreme feedback device aggregates the current status of jobs contained in a single view. Just collect all jobs to be monitored in a single Jenkins view and up you go! You can have multiple views, each controlling its own set of bears.

The bear lamps are updated only once per minute. This interval protects the energy saving lamps inside the bears from being switched too rapidly.

ColorMeaning
BlueAll builds are fine.
YellowAt least one job is currently building.
RedAt least one job has been built unsuccessfully or is unstable.
